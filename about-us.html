The Revival
Endometriosis Group

We're a community-based organization born from the lived experiences of women facing endometriosis in Uganda, Rubanda District.

We saw a gap where formal systems struggled, leaving patients isolated and financially burdened.

Our mission is to fill that void by providing holistic support, advocacy, and a pathway to resilience so no woman has to face this chronic condition alone.

Our
Core Goals

01

Empower Through Education and Awareness

To combat long diagnostic delays by raising awareness among the public, patients, and local healthcare providers about the symptoms, severity, and reality of endometriosis.

02

Provide Comprehensive Psycho-Social Support

To create a safe, non-judgmental community that actively fights the stigma, isolation, and depression associated with chronic pain and infertility.

03

Foster Financial Resilience

To mitigate the economic burden of endometriosis by offering affordable loans and resources, enabling members to manage medical costs and regain financial stability.

04

Advocate for Better Access to Care

To build partnerships with healthcare providers, policymakers, and other organizations to lobby for more accessible, specialized, and affordable treatment options locally.
